Sulake is a game development company specialized in massively multiplayer online games and communities. Our games, such as Habbo Hotel and Coke Studios, are community-driven, readily accessible and easy to play. These factors are the core of all Sulake products.
 
 
Sulake currently has 70 employees and operates in nine countries: Finland, Germany, Italy, Japan, The Netherlands, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland and the United Kingdom.
